A Visit to the English Language Academy

Written By: Imnil Benmarc A. Jolo

In Tacloban City, Leyte, the largest city in the Eastern Visayas region, the RCIS team visited the English Language Academy. This visit was an opportunity to connect with aspiring students and offer them guidance on both language proficiency and the pathways available for studying or working abroad. The team provided valuable insights into how enhancing English skills through the IELTS exam can open doors to global opportunities, especially in Canada, where language proficiency plays a key role in immigration processes.
