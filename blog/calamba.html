LAGUNA ALL THE WAY

Written By: Imnil Benmarc A. Jolo

Connecting with Students in Calamba

In Calamba, Laguna, the regional center of the CALABARZON region, the team visited the branch of the Niner Review and Testing Center. There, they had the chance to engage with students preparing for their exams, discussing strategies for success in the IELTS test and how achieving higher scores can help them unlock greater opportunities in education and immigration. This visit also highlighted the importance of continuous learning and preparation for students aiming to pursue their dreams of studying, working, or living in Canada.
