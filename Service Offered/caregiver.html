Caregiver Permit

A caregiver provides personal care and support to individuals in need, such as the elderly or those with disabilities. They assist with daily activities while promoting dignity and independence.

About

The Caregiver Pilot Program is designed for individuals who wish to immigrate to Canada to work as in-home caregivers. It provides a pathway to permanent residency for caregivers who meet certain eligibility criteria and have a valid job offer.

Criteria

  • Basic Language Proficiency: CLB 5+ (English/French)
  • Valid Job Offer: Full-time caregiver offer in Canada
  • Work Experience: 24 months in past 36 months (if already in Canada)
  • Education: Equivalent to Canadian post-secondary 1-year credential

Step by Step Process

  1. Employer Submits Offer: Employer submits job offer and proof.
  2. Apply for Work Permit: Submit online application with all documents.
  3. Biometrics: Attend biometrics appointment.
  4. Medical Exam: Complete your medical examination.
  5. Receive Permit: Once approved, start working in Canada.

Required Documents

  • Valid Passport
  • Job Offer Letter
  • Proof of Education
  • Language Test Results
  • Medical and Police Clearance
  • Proof of Work Experience

FAQs

  • Q: Can I bring my family?
    A: Yes, dependents may accompany you.
  • Q: Do I need a job offer before applying?
    A: Yes, a valid offer is required.
  • Q: Can I apply from outside Canada?
    A: Yes, international applicants are eligible.
